Aim 2: Predict Energy from micronutrients only (macros & fatty-acid detail excluded)

Interpretation: Models use vitamins, minerals, carotenoids, choline, etc., but not
protein, fat, carbohydrate, sugars, water, fiber, alcohol, or individual SFA/MUFA/PUFA
chains — so R² is expected to be modest versus macro-based models. This isolates how
much caloric level is linearly/nonlinearly associated with micronutrient patterns, which
supports follow-up work (e.g. ranking foods by micronutrient density = sum of scaled
micronutrients per kcal, or studying residuals from these models).
